Privacy Policy — SnapLink (Screenshot to Link)

Chrome Web Store item: SnapLink — Screenshot to Link
Operated by: MEMZO.DEV
Last updated: 22 June 2026
Contact: support@memzo.dev

This Privacy Policy explains what data the SnapLink Chrome extension (the "Extension") collects, how it is used, where and how it is stored, how long it is kept, who it is shared with, and the choices and rights you have. SnapLink has a single purpose: to let you capture a screenshot and generate a shareable link for it. We only collect data needed to deliver that purpose.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the following categories of data, and only when you take an action that requires it:

DataWhen collectedWhy
Account / authentication data — your email address and authentication tokens (and basic profile info such as name if you sign in with Google). When you create an account or sign in. To create your personal account, authenticate you, and link your screenshots to you.
Screenshots / captured content — the image you capture and any page region it contains. Only when you click the capture button. Capture is never automatic. To generate and store the shareable link you requested.
Link & item metadata — generated link ID, title, timestamps, and visibility setting of each capture. When a capture is saved. To let you view, manage, and share your captures.
Technical/log data — IP address, browser/extension version, and request timestamps. Automatically when the Extension communicates with our servers. Security, abuse prevention, and reliability. Not used for advertising or profiling.

The Extension does not read, collect, or transmit the content of web pages you visit, your browsing history, keystrokes, or form data. It does not run in the background to monitor your activity.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To capture, upload, store, and serve the screenshots you create.
  • To generate and manage your shareable links.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the service and prevent abuse.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data. We do not use your data for any purpose unrelated to SnapLink's single purpose stated above.

3. How and Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Screenshots are stored as files in Cloudflare R2 object storage.
  • Account data and metadata are stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database.
  • Application/API requests are processed by our backend hosted on Railway.
  • All data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS/TLS. Stored data is protected by the access controls and encryption-at-rest provided by the above providers.
  • Authentication credentials are handled by Supabase Auth; we never store your password in plain text.

4. How Your Data Is Shared

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with the service providers (sub-processors) strictly necessary to run SnapLink:

ProviderPurposeData involved
Cloudflare (R2 + CDN)Screenshot storage & deliveryScreenshot images, link IDs
SupabaseDatabase & authenticationAccount data, metadata
RailwayBackend/API hostingRequest/log data

We may also disclose data if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or security of our users or the service.

Shareable links

When you create a shareable link, anyone who has that link can view the screenshot without signing in, unless you set the capture to private or delete it. Please do not capture sensitive information you do not wish to be viewable by anyone holding the link.

5. Data Retention

We keep your screenshots, links, and account data for as long as your account is active or until you delete them. When you delete a capture, the underlying image and metadata are removed from our active systems. When you delete your account, your associated personal data and stored screenshots are deleted. Backups and logs are retained for a limited period and then purged.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access / export: you can view your saved captures in your account at any time.
  • Delete: you can delete any individual capture, or request deletion of your entire account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw: you can uninstall the Extension at any time, which stops all further data collection.

To exercise any of these rights, or to request full deletion, email support@memzo.dev and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Local Storage in the Extension

The Extension uses your browser's local extension storage to keep you signed in and to store your preferences. This data stays in your browser and is not used for tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

SnapLink is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, contact support@memzo.dev.
